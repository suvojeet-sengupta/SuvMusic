SuvMusic is a beautiful, open-source YouTube Music client with a sleek, minimalist user interface. Designed focusing on aesthetics and user privacy, it requires zero external trackers and lets you stream your favorite music completely ad-free.

Key Features:
• Completely free and open-source (GPL-3.0)
• Absolutely no ads or telemetrics
• Beautiful, modern Material You inspired design
• Stream songs natively from YouTube Music
• Synchronized lyrics via LrcLib
• Customizable themes and fluid animations
• Sleep timer
• Automated crash reporting securely via ACRA (can be disabled)

Built efficiently without unnecessary bloat, SuvMusic respects your system resources while offering a premium online listening experience.
