SuvMusic is a beautiful, open-source offline music and video player with a sleek, minimalist user interface. Designed focusing on aesthetics and user privacy, it requires zero external trackers to function natively playing your local media, yet supports robust integrations with custom plugins.

Key Features:
• Completely free and open-source (GPL-3.0)
• Absolutely no ads or telemetrics
• Beautiful, modern Material You inspired design
• Plays your local offline audio and video seamlessly
• Search and stream content with custom plugin support
• Synchronized lyrics via LrcLib
• Customizable themes and fluid animations
• Sleep timer
• Automated crash reporting securely via ACRA (can be disabled)

Built efficiently without unnecessary bloat, SuvMusic respects your system resources while offering a premium listening experience.
